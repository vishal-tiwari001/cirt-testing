What You Will Master

Every pillar is built around decisions your team already makes — AIS-190 submissions, OTA campaign reviews, audit queries, evidence documentation. This program makes you the person in the room who knows exactly what to do.

Pillar 01 — Regulatory Compliance

Know What CIRT Expects Before the Auditor Walks In

When your team drafts an AIS-190 submission, you will know exactly what format passes and what gets flagged — because you learned it from the body that reviews it. Gap analysis, audit response frameworks, UN R156 documentation structure — skills that apply to every regulatory interaction your team handles.

Pillar 02 — Technical Implementation

Build and Review OTA Systems With Regulatory Confidence

Design PKI infrastructures and OTA/FOTA pipelines using the same architecture patterns that have passed Type Approval for production vehicles on Indian roads. When a technical decision lands on your desk — a PKI config review, an OTA campaign flag — you will have the regulatory rationale to back your call.

Pillar 03 — Operational Excellence

Run Live SUMS Campaigns the Way Production Systems Demand

RxSWIN generation, rollback strategy, campaign validation gates — these are the daily decisions your team makes once SUMS goes live. You will learn to operate them as a real production system with proper state management, not treat them as a compliance checkbox that gets filled once and forgotten.

Pillar 04 — Evidence Generation

Produce Documentation That Passes Type Approval the First Time

Leave with the exact evidence structure CIRT expects at submission — not a template you adapt later, but an actual pack assembled in a live lab with an auditor in the room. Audit trails, assessor-ready reports, SUMS documentation formatting — built during this program, ready to use on your next submission.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Who is this program designed for?

Exclusively for CSMS-certified professionals (ISO 21434 / UN R155) — OEM engineers, product security teams, Tier-1/2 supplier engineers, compliance & regulatory officers, automotive cybersecurity professionals, and backend/embedded engineers building OTA infrastructure. This is an Advanced Implementation Course, not an Introduction.

Is this suitable for non-coders?

Yes. The program focuses on regulatory implementation, audit-ready documentation, and evidence pack assembly — not coding. Technical sessions use guided labs with pre-built tooling so participants from all backgrounds can follow along confidently.

What format is the program delivered in?

The program runs in a Self-paced online modules (12.5 hrs); Live online training (20 hrs) with interactive labs; Online query clinics (6 hrs); and optional in-person live labs at CIRT Pune on Day 4. Fully remote attendance is supported.

What certification do I receive?

Upon passing the final competency assessment (06 Jun 2026), you receive an official CIRT Certification — issued by the Central Institute of Road Transport, India's government Type Approval Body. The only SUMS Advanced certification backed by a statutory regulatory authority.

Are all materials included in the fee?

Yes. The fee of ₹45,000 + GST per participant covers video access, labs, evidence pack templates, RxSWIN strategy documentation, query clinic access, and the CIRT certification exam. No hidden extras. Video library access is available for the duration of the program timeline.

What are the prerequisites?

Participants should ideally hold CSMS certification or have equivalent understanding of automotive cybersecurity fundamentals, familiarity with AIS-189/AIS-190 standards, and experience with software update management systems. PKI/OTA background is helpful but not mandatory.
